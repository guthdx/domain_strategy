### Sitemap Strategy
Each site needs a simple sitemap.xml:
```xml
<?xml version="1.0" encoding="UTF-8"?>
<urlset xmlns="http://www.sitemaps.org/schemas/sitemap/0.9">
  <url>
    <loc>https://DOMAIN/</loc>
    <lastmod>2025-12-19</lastmod>
    <priority>1.0</priority>
  </url>
</urlset>
```

## Phase 3: Marketplace Listing

### Marketplace Strategy

Based on research, use a **multi-platform approach**:

#### Primary Platforms

1. **Afternic** (GoDaddy network)
   - Largest distribution network (80+ registrar partners)
   - Fast Transfer technology (80% faster transactions)
   - Best for: Quick sales, $1k-$10k domains
   - Commission: 15-20%
   - List: ALL domains

2. **Sedo**
   - Largest global marketplace (18M+ domains)
   - Best for high-value sales ($5k+)
   - Broker assistance available (22% higher prices)
   - Commission: 15-20%
   - Average sale time: 92 days
   - List: royalsmartpeople.com, g3dx.com, doctorswithinborders.org

3. **Dan.com**
   - Modern UX, fast transactions
   - Installment payment options
   - Lower fees than competitors
   - Commission: 9%
   - List: ALL domains

#### Secondary Platforms

4. **Squadhelp**
   - Curated marketplace for brandable domains
   - Good for creative names
   - List: guthed.com, inertialism.com

5. **GoDaddy Auctions**
   - Massive buyer base
   - 28-day average sale time for $1k-$5k range
   - Commission: 25%
   - Use for: Domains not selling elsewhere after 90 days

### Listing Priority

**Week 1**: Afternic + Dan.com (all 8 domains)
**Week 2**: Sedo (top 3 high-value domains)
**Week 3**: Squadhelp (creative domains)
**Month 3**: GoDaddy Auctions (any unsold)

## Risk Factors

1. **Market Timing**: Domain market fluctuates; patience required
2. **Trademark Issues**: doctorswithinborders.org may face challenges (similar to Doctors Without Borders/MSF)
3. **Buyer Finding**: Niche domains take longer to find right buyer
4. **Price Expectations**: May need to adjust targets based on market response

### Mitigation

- List on multiple platforms for maximum exposure
- Set realistic minimums (current appraised values)
- Be patient (average sale takes 60-120 days)
- Consider broker for high-value domains
